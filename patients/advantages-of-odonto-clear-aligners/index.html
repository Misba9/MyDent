Advantages of My Dent

My Dent clear aligners treatment is provided at a registered dental clinic by a qualified and experienced dentist only.

Our Treatment protocol is based on our experience of treatments provided to more than a million patients.

You can see the result

The virtual set up treatment plan, which is a 3D treatment planning, will allow you to visualise the final position of your teeth at the end of your treatment course.

No restrictions on what to eat/drink

You can usually eat whatever you desire while in treatment because the SDalign are removed when eating/drinking. Thus, there is no need to refrain from your favourite foods and drinks unless instructed by your doctor.

Virtually invisible

My Dent are virtually invisible and discreet, so most people will not even notice that you are wearing them.

No Damage to the teeth if Aligner breaks/gets defected

Contact your doctor if you have lost or broken an aligner. As your doctor may recommend that you temporarily go back one stage while a replacement is being made, it may be helpful to keep the most recently used My Dent.

Safe and Removable

My Dent clear aligners are removable so you can brush & floss your teeth & maintain good oral hygiene. Traditional braces come with problems like bits of food trapped in the wires and brackets, leading to tooth decay and gum disease. My Dent clear aligners are removable so you can continue to eat and drink whatever you want, and take part in sports and similar activities.

Rapid Results

Compared to other teeth-straightening methods, My Dent works fast. On average, total treatment time with My Dent is 12-18 months, and many people notice results in a matter of weeks.

Feel Better about Yourself

Finally, and most importantly to me, is that OODNTO quickly help people to feel better about themselves because they are invisible and help in correcting the smiles without being noticed and having to stop anything.
